# Getting started

This section is meant for users who are getting hands-on with their
analog device for the first time. Step by step, we will walk you
through:

- [setting up](./setup.md) the `pybrid` packages in a Python
  environment,
- [connecting to your device](./identifying-your-device.md) over the
  network,
- [building and running](./building-your-first-circuit.md) your first
  analog circuit on the hardware.

This guide specifically does not cover the hardware-side setup, such
as setting up the device itself, connecting it to the network, or
updating its firmware. For those steps, please refer to the
integrated manual that ships with your device.

Before starting, please read through the
[requirements](./requirements.md) and check that they are satisfied
on your system.
